## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Calculator Tab

### Input Fields

**Monthly Rent**
- Dollar amount (e.g., $2,500)
- Used as base for calculation

**Commission Rate (%)**
- Percentage of monthly rent (e.g., 15%)
- Quick preset buttons: 10%, 12%, 15%, 20%
- Custom values supported

**Brokerage Split (%)**
- Percentage that goes to brokerage (e.g., 50%)
- Shows you keep the remaining percentage
- Adjustable in 5% increments

### Results Display (4 Cards)

**Total Commission** (Purple)
- Shows full commission amount
- Breakdown: X% of monthly rent

**Brokerage Fee** (Red)
- Amount deducted for brokerage
- Shows split percentage

**Your Commission** (Green)
- Your take-home amount
- Your percentage of total

**Net Earnings** (Yellow)
- Final amount after split
- Same as Your Commission

### Annual Projection (4 Scenarios)
- 5 deals/year
- 10 deals/year
- 20 deals/year
- 50 deals/year

### Export Button
- Downloads calculation as text file
- Includes all details and breakdown

## üî¢ Calculations

### Commission Formula
```
Total Commission = Monthly Rent √ó (Commission Rate / 100)
Brokerage Fee = Total Commission √ó (Brokerage Split / 100)
Your Commission = Total Commission - Brokerage Fee
Net Earnings = Your Commission
```

### Example
```
Monthly Rent: $2,500
Commission Rate: 15%
Brokerage Split: 50%

Total: $2,500 √ó 0.15 = $375
Brokerage: $375 √ó 0.50 = $187.50
Your Take: $375 - $187.50 = $187.50
```
